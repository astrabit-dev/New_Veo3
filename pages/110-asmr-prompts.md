# 92. ASMR·힐링 프롬프트 20선

## ASMR·힐링 프롬프트 작성 원칙
- 소리를 먼저 생각하고 영상을 구성
- 느리고 의도적인 움직임
- 클로즈업과 매크로 촬영
- 순수하고 방해 없는 감각 경험

---

**#1 이슬 맺힌 거미줄**
```
Extreme macro close-up of a perfect spider's web in early morning light, every thread carrying dozens of dew drops that catch the rising sun like diamonds. A very gentle breeze causes the web to sway almost imperceptibly. The camera moves micro-millimeters across the web, exploring the geometry of each drop. Audio: absolute silence except for the most distant birdsong and the faintest possible sound of air. Soft golden sunrise light. Hyper-detailed macro photography, ultra calm.
```

**#2 모래 흘리기**
```
Fine white beach sand being poured very slowly from one cupped hand to another, filmed in extreme slow motion. The sand flows like liquid, individual grains visible, each catching light differently. The flow is perfectly controlled — a thin, even stream. The sand makes a soft hushing sound as it falls. A small mountain of sand grows below, then is allowed to fall away. The hands are still, unhurried. Bright neutral light, ultra sharp macro. Pure, simple, satisfying.
```

**#3 꿀 떨어뜨리기**
```
Extremely slow macro shot of golden honey being dripped from a dipper onto a thick slice of toast. The honey falls in a perfectly smooth, unbroken stream — amber and transparent where it catches the backlight, deep gold where it pools. It fans out slowly on the bread, flowing into the valleys of the toast texture. The dipper is held completely still. Audio: the very quiet, very satisfying sound of honey landing and spreading. Warm morning light. Hyper-real, no music, no voice.
```

**#4 대나무 빗속**
```
A bamboo grove in gentle rain. The rain falls in even, soft curtains through the green stems. Each leaf channels the water into small streams that meet and drip. The bamboo makes a sound — a deep, resonant knock when drops collect and the leaves bob — a natural percussion. The camera moves very slowly through the grove at ground level. Everything is a hundred shades of green. Audio: rain on bamboo, the low knocking, birds somewhere high above. Serene, natural, deeply calming.
```

**#5 도자기 만들기**
```
Close-up of hands centering a lump of dark grey clay on a pottery wheel. The clay responds to the pressure — rising, narrowing, widening. The hands are wet, completely focused. The wheel turns at a steady, meditative pace. The clay takes a form slowly — first rough, then refined. Audio: the smooth hum of the wheel, the wet slide of clay, the potter's quiet breathing. Natural studio light. Simple, focused, completely present. ASMR quality audio.
```

**#6 나뭇잎 위의 빗방울**
```
Macro footage of large, perfect raindrops landing on a broad monstera leaf — each drop lands and creates a perfect circular ripple across the leaf's surface, then rolls slowly to the edge. The leaf is so waxy that the drops remain perfectly spherical until they fall. Three drops race each other to the leaf's edge. Audio: the clean, delicate tap of each raindrop, the rolling sounds as they slide. Deep green, white light, extraordinary macro detail. Peaceful, rhythmic, inevitable.
```

**#7 은빛 실 짜기**
```
Extreme close-up of fine silver thread being woven on a small hand loom. The threads are almost hair-fine, catching light like fiber optics. Fingers guide each thread with practiced precision — over, under, over, under. The fabric taking shape has a texture like solidified moonlight. Audio: the very quiet rhythm of the loom, the thread sliding against thread, the gentle tension and release. Soft diffused light. Slow, patterned, deeply satisfying.
```

**#8 불꽃 명상**
```
A single large candle in complete darkness, the flame absolutely still in the still air. Macro close-up allows us to see the fluid movement within the flame — how it breathes, how the colors layer from blue at the base through yellow to an almost white tip. The wax at the edge slowly liquefies and flows. The flame casts warm moving shadows on everything. Audio: only the almost-imperceptible crackle of the burning wick. Complete silence otherwise. Meditative. The flame is alive.
```

**#9 소금 결정 만들기**
```
Time-lapse macro of salt crystals growing in a supersaturated solution — small, clear squares forming and stacking in perfect geometric patterns on the glass surface. They grow from nothing, add edges, become three-dimensional. The process takes hours; the time-lapse makes it visible in real time. Audio: ambient laboratory quiet, the faintest sound of crystalline structure forming, which may be imaginary but feels real. Pure macro science made beautiful.
```

**#10 모닥불 침묵**
```
A campfire at night, outdoors, only the fire visible in a circle of warmth. The fire is large enough to be serious — wood crackling, flames dancing, sending sparks upward into the dark above where they disappear. The camera is close, at fire level, the flames filling the frame. Audio: the full campfire sound — crackling, the deeper bass notes of the logs, the pop of pockets of sap, the occasional sizzle of moisture. No music. No voice. Just fire in the dark. Pure and ancient.
```

**#11 파도 소리**
```
Extreme slow motion of a wave arriving on a pebble beach — the foam spreading forward over round smooth stones, filling every gap, then retreating with the characteristic rattling-rushing sound of pebbles in surf. Each pebble is wet, glistening, a different shade of grey, brown, white. The foam is pure white, complex, full of tiny bubbles. The motion is inexorable and gentle. Audio: the full spectrum of the wave — the building rush, the arrival, the pebble retreat. Rhythmic, inevitable, the sound of the ocean breathing.
```

**#12 찻잎 우러나기**
```
A glass teapot of clear hot water. A single dried tea leaf placed in the water. Time-lapse macro as the leaf slowly opens — unfurling from a tight curl into its full shape over several minutes, while simultaneously releasing color into the water around it — amber tendrils spreading in slow, beautiful patterns through the clear water, convecting gently. The water around the leaf gradually deepens from clear to golden to amber. Audio: the faintest sound of water moving, almost imperceptible, completely calming.
```

**#13 조개껍데기 수집**
```
Hands sorting through a collection of small beach shells on a white surface — picking up each one to examine it: the pearlescent interior of a nautilus, the perfect spiral of a snail shell, a small bivalve still intact. Each is placed carefully, creating an arrangement. The shells make a soft clink against each other. Audio: the quiet clinks of shell on shell, distant ocean sound, birds. Bright natural light. Calm, methodical, the meditative work of sorting beautiful things.
```

**#14 새벽 숲 걷기**
```
Walking slowly through a deciduous forest at early dawn, before sunrise — the light is that extraordinary blue-grey of almost-morning, the mist hangs low, the birds are just beginning. The camera is at walking pace, unhurried, moving between the trees. Dew on every surface. A deer, barely visible in the mist, watches from twenty meters away. Audio: footsteps on soft leaves, the early birds, the absolute quiet of the world before the day begins. Peaceful, fresh, alive.
```

**#15 비눗방울 터지기**
```
Extreme ultra-slow macro of soap bubbles bursting — the bubble exists for a fraction of a second in super slow motion: its rainbow iridescent surface, the way light plays across it, then the instant of rupture — the surface tension gives way at one point, and the entire structure collapses inward in a microsecond shower of infinitesimal droplets, each catching the light, dispersing. Audio: in real time, the near-silent pop of a bubble is barely there. At this scale, in silence, it's everything.
```

**#16 얼음 속 꽃**
```
A flower perfectly preserved in a large block of clear ice — the petals intact, colors vivid through the lens of ice. The block sits in warm studio light, and the ice is very, very slowly melting — tiny rivulets run down the outside, the surface loses its perfect clarity in tiny increments. The flower gets slowly closer. Audio: the near-silent sound of ice melting, the occasional drop of water from the bottom of the block onto the surface below. Time and beauty, together.
```

**#17 빵 굽는 소리**
```
A loaf of artisan bread fresh from the oven resting on a wooden board. The crust makes sounds as it cools — small cracking, settling sounds, the bread adjusting to the air temperature. This is called "singing" bread. Audio: only these sounds — small, structural, peaceful, like a wooden house settling at night. The crust is dark, slightly blistered, extraordinary in texture in extreme close-up. Warm kitchen light. The smell is implied in every frame.
```

**#18 수면 위 잎사귀**
```
A single large leaf floating on a completely still pond surface, filmed from above in extreme close-up. Tiny water striders walk across the water nearby, each foot creating a tiny dimple in the surface tension. The leaf turns infinitesimally slowly in some unfelt micro-current. Occasionally, a single bubble rises from below and pops at the surface. Audio: absolute silence, then the near-subliminal pop of the bubble. The water surface is perfect, still, a mirror except for these tiny living interruptions.
```

**#19 눈 내리는 창문**
```
Looking out a frosted window at snow falling outside. The snowflakes near the glass are large enough to see their shapes before they melt. Inside, the warmth slightly fogs the lower edges of the glass. A mug of something hot on the windowsill steams gently. Outside: complete, beautiful, silencing white. Audio: near silence — the snow absorbs sound. The faintest distant sound of a city under snow. The mug's warmth audible somehow in the framing. Comfort made visible.
```

**#20 모래 위 파문**
```
ASMR kinetic sand being slowly dragged by a wide-toothed comb across a flat surface — the sand falls in perfect ridges behind the comb, creating a Zen garden pattern. The comb moves with absolute evenness. The ridges are perfectly parallel. Then the comb is laid down, and a hand runs flat across the sand — all the ridges collapse back into a flat field. The process begins again. Audio: the sound of the sand moving under the comb — soft, granular, deeply tactile. Pure repetitive satisfaction.
```
