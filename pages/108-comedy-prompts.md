# 90. 코미디·유머 프롬프트 20선

## 코미디 프롬프트 작성 원칙
- 상황의 아이러니와 대조가 핵심
- 진지한 상황에 황당한 요소 삽입
- 캐릭터의 반응이 유머를 완성
- 예상 밖의 결말이 웃음을 만든다

---

**#1 빅풋의 온라인 쇼핑**
```
Bigfoot sits hunched over a comically small laptop at a desk, wearing reading glasses, scrolling an online shopping site. The screen shows: "Size 32 shoes — Sorry, out of stock." He scrolls down. "Size 33 — Out of stock." He sighs loudly — a huge foggy breath fills the room. His fingers are too big for the keyboard. He types "W" and accidentally hits six surrounding keys. He looks at the camera with profound, relatable frustration.
```

**#2 좀비 요가 클래스**
```
A yoga class in a bright studio, entirely composed of zombies. They're all doing their best — but zombie flexibility is limited. One falls over in Warrior II. The instructor (also a zombie, but a very centered one) says: "Remember, it's not about perfection. It's about the journey." The zombie in the front row has lost an arm during Downward Dog. She just continues with one arm, totally committed. Upbeat wellness music.
```

**#3 외계인의 첫 편의점 방문**
```
A small grey alien with huge eyes walks into a Korean convenience store at midnight, wearing an oversized hoodie to blend in. He picks up a Triangle Kimbap, examines it with scientific intensity, then holds it up to his large head for a moment. The cashier — a bored college student — doesn't even look up. The alien takes a ramen cup, adds hot water from the machine, sits at the counter, and eats it with four fingers, watching the street outside with fascination. The cashier finally glances over. Looks back at his phone.
```

**#4 기사의 현대 레스토랑 방문**
```
A knight in full plate armor sits at a modern fine dining restaurant. The waiter describes the evening's tasting menu in elegant detail. The knight attempts to eat — the fork scratches against his visor, his gauntlets are too large for the delicate cutlery. He manages a tiny portion of the first course and gives a thumbs up that clanks loudly on the metal armor. Other diners try politely not to stare. The knight asks for the menu in Old English.
```

**#5 공룡 직장인**
```
A T-Rex in a business suit attempts to type an email in a modern open-plan office. His arms are too short to reach the keyboard properly. He gets around this by using a pencil in his mouth. It's working remarkably well — he's actually quite fast. His coworkers chat around him as if this is completely normal. He takes a sip of his coffee, which is the size of a thimble to him but he holds it very carefully. He has a "World's Best Boss" mug that is approximately normal human-sized, tiny in his view.
```

**#6 빅풋 뷰티 유튜브**
```
Bigfoot sits in front of a ring light with a perfect beauty YouTube setup — multiple cameras, products arranged beautifully, professional lighting. He addresses the camera confidently: "Hey guys, welcome back to the channel! Today we're doing my full forest survival skincare routine." He holds up a mud product. "This is my daily face mask, which I find in the creek bed behind the clearing. I really feel like it evens out my complexion under all this fur." He applies it enthusiastically. His assistant (a small deer that just wandered in) watches from the doorway.
```

**#7 로봇의 첫 날**
```
A humanoid robot on its first day at an office job, dressed in office clothes (tie slightly off-center from having put it on himself). He is trying extremely hard to fit in. Someone makes a joke around the water cooler — the robot laughs at perfect comedic timing, exactly 1.3 seconds after everyone else. He then writes "LAUGH" in his notebook. Later: a coworker brings birthday cake. The robot doesn't eat, obviously, but takes a slice and carries it back to his desk where he stares at it for the rest of the afternoon, trying to seem normal.
```

**#8 예티의 온천**
```
The Yeti — enormous, white-furred — soaks in a traditional Japanese outdoor onsen, a tiny white towel folded perfectly on his head as is custom. He is absolutely blissful. He sighs contentedly, sending fog everywhere. A tiny elderly Japanese man gets into the onsen on the other side, doesn't bat an eye, settles in. The Yeti greets him politely in broken Japanese. The old man responds in kind. They sit in comfortable hot springs silence. Two strangers sharing a perfect moment.
```

**#9 드래곤의 요리 대결**
```
A cooking competition. The camera reveals the contestants: four human chefs and one enormous dragon. The dragon's station is significantly larger. Announcer: "And today's mystery ingredient is... crème brûlée." The dragon looks at his blowtorch. Looks at himself. Looks at the camera. He very carefully breathes the tiniest, most controlled flame onto the sugar surface of the dessert. Perfect caramelization. He looks up, immensely proud of himself. The judges are trying very hard not to faint.
```

**#10 외계인 관광객**
```
Three aliens — dressed in what they clearly believe are normal human tourist clothes (absolutely not normal) — walk through a Seoul street market consulting a guidebook. One holds the book upside down. They stop at a tteokbokki stall. The vendor, utterly unfazed, asks what they want. The aliens confer in clicks and beeps, pointing at various items. The vendor nods and starts cooking. This is just Tuesday. They eat with instruments they brought themselves, which get the job done surprisingly well. They give the vendor a five-star review on their home planet's Yelp equivalent.
```

**#11 고양이의 회의**
```
A boardroom meeting. Twelve cats sit around a conference table in business attire, some on laptop keyboards (obviously). The CEO cat, a large ginger tabby, is presenting a PowerPoint. The screen shows complex graphs. Three cats at the back are batting at each other. One has knocked its water glass off the table and is watching it fall in slow motion with absolute focus. The CEO cat sighs. One cat in the front row is aggressively kneading the table. All the phones on the table start vibrating with a bird video. The meeting rapidly devolves.
```

**#12 용이 드라이빙 스쿨**
```
A driving instructor sits very stiffly in the passenger seat as a dragon — wings folded awkwardly inside the car, tail somehow squished into the back seat — attempts to parallel park. The car wobbles slightly every time the dragon moves. The dragon is actually very careful and considerate. They signal every turn. But the car is slightly on fire on the left side, which both driver and instructor are pretending not to notice. The instructor makes a note on his clipboard: "Needs work on spatial awareness."
```

**#13 빅풋 집들이**
```
Bigfoot hosts a housewarming party in his forest cave. He's decorated with fairy lights strung on stalactites. He's made a charcuterie board from forest berries and what appears to be a very large pinecone. His guests: two deer, a bear who's trying very hard to behave, and three raccoons who are immediately into everything. Bigfoot nervously hovers, offering snacks from a very large bowl. The raccoons have found the cheese. The bear is sitting on his best rock. He smiles and decides it's all fine.
```

**#14 중세 기사의 GPS**
```
A knight on horseback stops at a crossroads in medieval England. He holds up a glowing magic orb — a GPS equivalent. The voice from the orb says, politely: "Turn left at the mill." He turns left. "Recalculating. Turn left at the mill." He's gone in a circle. He looks at the orb with deep frustration. "I said LEFT at the MILL." He looks at his left. Then the other left. "Which left?" The orb sighs. "I cannot believe this is happening again."
```

**#15 해저 카페**
```
A charming café on the ocean floor. Regular human customers sit at tables, in diving suits, reading menus. A mermaid waitress takes orders on a waterproof notepad. The specials board (written in waterproof chalk) includes: "Today's special: Plankton Toast." A customer's coffee floats out of their cup and drifts upward — they catch it and press it back in. A fish swims through the café. Nobody looks up. The mermaid brings the check. Accepts payment in both regular money and interesting shells.
```

**#16 좀비 직업 박람회**
```
A job fair for zombies. Booths from various companies: "Brain Industry Ltd.", "Shuffling Logistics," "International Moaning Corp." Zombies in their best business casual (somewhat tattered) shuffle from booth to booth with resumes. A career counselor zombie helps a young zombie polish his resume: "I see here you have 'ate a lot of people.' We should probably rephrase that as 'aggressive client acquisition.'" The young zombie nods, writes it down slowly.
```

**#17 빅풋의 데이팅 앱**
```
Bigfoot lies on his back in the forest, holding his phone above his face, creating a dating profile. Profile photo attempts: too close, blurry, accidentally photographs a squirrel, accidentally photographs his own nostril. He settles on a distant shot. His bio reads: "Outdoorsy. Into long walks. Prefer one-on-one meetings. Prefer you not tell anyone I exist. 8 feet tall, love hiking, very warm." He swipes for a while. Comes across a Yeti profile. Pauses. Swipes right immediately. Then immediately nervous. His phone buzzes — it's a match. He screams so loud that birds leave the trees for miles.
```

**#18 마법사의 IT 지원**
```
A medieval wizard sits impatiently at his ancient stone desk while a very young, very modern IT support person attempts to set up his new "crystal ball upgrade." The IT person is very professional. The wizard is deeply suspicious of all of it. "But will it run Scrying 3.0?" "Sir, that's not really a— " "I need to run Scrying 3.0. And can it connect to the Prophecy Printer?" "I don't... know what a Prophecy Printer is." The wizard opens a drawer and shows a printer made of bones and moonstone. The IT person looks at it for a long moment. Types something into their support system.
```

**#19 용의 반려동물 등록**
```
A dragon the size of a house stands politely in line at the local pet registration office. The line is: a woman with a cat, a man with a standard poodle, another woman with a rabbit, and then an enormous dragon. When it's the dragon's turn, the municipal clerk — who has seen everything — doesn't blink. "Name of pet?" The dragon's owner, a young woman, says "Gerald." The clerk types "Gerald." "Species?" "Dragon." "Approximate weight?" Everyone in the office turns to look. The dragon shifts modestly. "He's between visits at the vet, we're not exactly sure." The clerk nods. Types.
```

**#20 고릴라 오케스트라**
```
A full symphony orchestra, entirely composed of gorillas, performing a classical concert. They are exceptional musicians. Absolute professionals. The audience of mixed humans and primates is completely captivated. During a pianissimo passage, one gorilla in the string section sneezes enormously — launches the music stand forward. The sheet music drifts across the stage. The conductor (a very dignified elder gorilla) stops the piece. Waits. The section picks up their music. Settle. The conductor raises his baton again. They resume as if nothing happened. Note-perfect.
```
