# 부록 F. 한국어 전용 고급 프롬프트 패턴

> **한국 문화·감성·미학에 특화된 프롬프트 — K-드라마, K-팝, 한국 일상**

---

## 왜 한국 특화 패턴이 필요한가

Veo 3는 한국어 입력과 한국 문화 콘텐츠를 잘 이해합니다. 하지만 서양 영화 스타일로 학습된 기본 패턴과 달리, 한국 특유의 미학을 끌어내려면 **한국 맥락에 특화된 키워드와 구조**가 필요합니다.

이 부록은 한국 콘텐츠 제작에서 검증된 패턴 모음입니다.

---

## 패턴 1: K-드라마 감성 장면

### 공식

```
[한국 드라마 캐릭터 묘사] + [한국적 배경] + [K-드라마 특유의 감정선]
+ [cinematic Korean drama aesthetic] + [드라마틱 조명]
```

### K-드라마 핵심 키워드

```
스타일: "Korean drama aesthetic", "K-drama cinematic style",
         "Korean melodrama visual style"

배경: "Han River bridge at night", "traditional Korean pojangmacha",
       "Seoul rooftop at golden hour", "Bukchon Hanok village alley",
       "Hongdae street at night", "convenience store at 3am"

감정: "longing gaze", "bittersweet expression", "suppressed tears",
       "unspoken feelings", "dramatic eye contact"

촬영: "slow motion", "rain effect", "close-up on eyes reflecting city lights",
       "wind in hair", "backlit silhouette"
```

### 실전 예시 1 — 이별 장면

```
A 28-year-old Korean woman, elegant in a long white coat,
stands alone on Han River bridge at night,
city lights reflecting on the dark water below,
her hair gently moved by cold autumn wind,
she looks down at her phone then slowly turns to face the city,
eyes glistening with unshed tears, lips pressed together,
extreme close-up of her face, then slow pull back revealing
the vast bridge and city behind her,
Korean melodrama cinematic style,
soft blue cold lighting, deep melancholic mood.
[Audio: soft piano melody, distant traffic, light wind, river sounds]
[Avoid: cheerful colors, background crowds, daytime lighting]
```

### 실전 예시 2 — 운명적 만남 (우연한 조우)

```
Two young Koreans, a man and a woman, both in their late 20s,
bump into each other at the entrance of a traditional pojangmacha
on a rainy Seoul night, the man catches her before she falls,
their eyes meet for the first time — frozen moment,
neon orange tent glow behind them, rain creating bokeh in background,
extreme slow-motion, close-up on their interlocked hands then up to eyes,
romantic K-drama first encounter aesthetic,
warm orange and cold blue color contrast.
[Audio: rain, distant street noise, soft romantic background score]
```

## 패턴 2: K-팝 퍼포먼스 & 뮤직비디오

### 공식

```
[아이돌 외형 묘사] + [퍼포먼스 환경] + [K-팝 MV 스타일 키워드]
+ [dynamic camera movement] + [specific lighting rig]
```

### K-팝 핵심 키워드

```
스타일: "K-pop music video aesthetic", "idol group performance style",
         "Korean pop MV cinematic", "K-pop concept visual"

컨셉별:
- 걸크러시: "girl crush concept, powerful choreography, fierce expression"
- 청순: "innocent concept, soft pastel lighting, gentle movements"
- 다크/미스테리: "dark concept, dramatic shadows, intense gaze"
- 레트로: "retro K-pop concept, film grain, vintage color grading"

퍼포먼스 공간:
"massive LED cube stage", "rooftop at sunset", "dark warehouse with spotlights",
"field of flowers", "school hallway concept set"
```

### 실전 예시 1 — 걸그룹 센터 샷

```
A young Korean female idol, early 20s, sharp features,
sleek black hair in a high ponytail, wearing a silver metallic
structured jacket and black high-waisted pants,
executes a powerful sharp dance move — arms snapping outward —
center stage in a massive black studio,
surrounded by geometric mirror panels reflecting light,
four backup dancers visible in periphery,
low angle shot looking up, dramatic single spotlight from above,
cool blue and silver color scheme,
K-pop girl crush MV aesthetic, high energy.
[Audio: no dialogue, deep bass ambient sound]
[Avoid: casual clothing, bright warm colors, static camera]
```

### 실전 예시 2 — 보이그룹 감성 씬

```
A Korean male idol in his early 20s, soft features,
bleached blond hair slightly disheveled, wearing an oversized
cream-colored vintage sweater, stands in an empty school hallway
at the end of the day, sunlight streaming through the window
creating long golden shadows on the tiled floor,
he leans against a locker and looks down at something off-screen,
nostalgic and wistful expression,
medium shot, film grain, warm golden hour color grading,
coming-of-age K-pop MV aesthetic, youth and longing.
[Audio: quiet school ambiance, distant footsteps fading away]
```

```
[구체적인 한국 일상 공간] + [일상적인 한국인 행동] + [시간대]
+ [감성 키워드] + [cinematic everyday Korean life style]
```

### 한국 일상 공간 키워드

```
새벽·밤: "Korean 24-hour convenience store (GS25/CU aesthetic)",
           "gwanaksan mountain trail at dawn", "subway station at 11pm",
           "pojangmacha tent on rainy night", "PC방 at midnight"

낮: "traditional Korean market (시장)", "school rooftop during lunch",
     "Noryangjin fish market morning", "Sinchon university street",
     "Han River park on sunny Sunday"

계절 특화:
- 봄: "Yeouido cherry blossom festival crowd"
- 여름: "Han River night picnic with tteokbokki"
- 가을: "Namsan Seoul Tower autumn leaves"
- 겨울: "Gwanghwamun Square heavy snowfall"
```

### 실전 예시 1 — 편의점 새벽

```
A young Korean man in his mid-20s, tired expression,
wearing a grey hooded sweatshirt and sweatpants,
sits alone on a plastic stool in front of a Korean convenience store
at 3am, eating instant cup ramyeon, steam rising,
fluorescent store light spilling warm yellow onto the cold street,
a few passing cars' headlights sweep across him,
he stares into the middle distance as he eats,
medium shot, handheld slight movement, Korean midnight solitude aesthetic,
cool blue night contrasting warm store interior light.
[Audio: slurping noodles, quiet street, distant convenience store
        jingle, faint car passing]
```

### 실전 예시 2 — 한강 피크닉

```
A group of four young Koreans, early 20s, mix of men and women,
spread out on a picnic mat on Han River park grass on a sunny afternoon,
surrounded by치킨 delivery boxes, cans of beer, and snacks,
they laugh and talk animatedly, one person plays guitar softly,
the Han River and Seoul skyline visible in the background,
wide shot slowly narrowing to medium, warm afternoon light,
candid documentary style, joyful and carefree Korean youth aesthetic.
[Audio: laughter, guitar melody, distant river sounds, breeze, city hum]
```

## 패턴 4: 한옥과 전통 미학

### 공식

```
[한옥 공간 묘사] + [전통 복식 or 현대-전통 믹스] + [시간대]
+ [Korean traditional aesthetic] + [자연 음향]
```

### 한국 전통 키워드

```
공간: "traditional Korean hanok courtyard (마당)", "Bukchon Hanok Village alley",
       "Gyeongbok Palace grounds", "Andong Hahoe folk village",
       "traditional Korean teahouse (찻집)"

의상:
- 전통: "wearing a silk hanbok in deep indigo and gold"
- 퓨전: "modern fusion hanbok, traditional silhouette with contemporary cut"

계절 요소:
- 봄: "paper doors (hanji) glowing in morning light, plum blossoms"
- 여름: "stone floor cooling in evening shade, locust tree sound"
- 가을: "persimmons drying under eave, fallen gingko leaves"
- 겨울: "snow settling on curved rooftile, stone wall"
```

### 실전 예시 — 한옥 새벽

```
A Korean woman in her 40s wearing a simple natural linen jeogori,
sits on the wooden floor (마루) of a traditional hanok,
both hands around a celadon tea bowl,
early morning fog in the courtyard beyond the open sliding door,
bare persimmon tree branches visible against the pale grey sky,
the silence is complete except for distant roosters,
wide shot from inside the room looking out through the doorway,
Korean traditional aesthetic, meditation and stillness,
soft pre-dawn blue-grey light, no artificial light.
[Audio: distant rooster, paper door creak, tea being sipped,
        complete outdoor silence]
[Avoid: modern elements, bright colors, any artificial lighting]
```
