# 93. 광고·마케팅 프롬프트 20선

## 광고 프롬프트 작성 원칙
- 제품을 영웅으로 만드는 조명
- 브랜드 무드를 일관되게 유지
- 8초 안에 제품 특성 전달
- 시청자의 욕망에 호소하는 분위기

---

**#1 럭셔리 시계**
```
A luxury men's watch sits on a polished black stone surface. The watch face shows hands at 10:10 — the classic display position. The watch has a deep navy blue dial with small complications, a stainless steel case, and a dark brown leather strap that shows the quality of its stitching in macro detail. The camera starts from below the surface level, slowly rising to reveal the watch from a dramatic low angle, then orbits slowly. Studio single overhead light creates one perfect shadow. The ticking of the mechanism is the only audio.
```

**#2 스포츠카 런칭**
```
A sleek silver sports car on an empty mountain road at first light — the car is parked at the edge of a hairpin turn, the whole valley visible below in the morning mist. No driver, just the car. The camera orbits it slowly, catching the curve of every body panel, the glint of light on the wheels, the red brake calipers visible through the spokes. Then the engine starts — the sound of it in this empty mountain air is extraordinary. Cinematic 16:9, dramatic golden hour, the car is everything.
```

**#3 프리미엄 커피**
```
A single espresso being drawn from a high-end espresso machine — the flow of coffee is perfect, rich brown with a copper crema forming. The shot glass fills slowly, the crema building, swirling. The glass is placed on a white saucer. The steam rises. A second camera shows the beans in macro — their roasted surface, the oils catching light. The cup is lifted and the crema breaks and reforms. No hands, no person. Just the coffee and the quality. Warm tone. Morning light. The sound of the machine, the coffee.
```

**#4 무선 이어폰**
```
A single wireless earbud floats slowly in a dark void, rotating to show all angles — smooth matte white, the speaker grille's precise mesh, the sensor lens. It is joined by the charging case, which opens to receive it — the magnetic click is satisfying and audible. The case glows with a charging indicator. The whole system floats together, rotating. Electric blue and white lighting. The product is the hero. Clean, precise, premium.
```

**#5 고급 스킨케어 세럼**
```
A small glass dropper bottle of facial serum on a white marble surface. The liquid inside is a pale gold — visible through the clear glass as slightly viscous, containing tiny light-catching particles. The dropper is squeezed over the back of a hand — a single drop forms, falls in extreme slow motion, lands with a tiny crown splash visible only in macro. The liquid spreads on the skin surface and is absorbed, leaving a barely-there glow. Audio: complete silence. Pure, premium, effective.
```

**#6 초콜릿 브랜드**
```
A premium dark chocolate bar on a wooden board — the deep brown surface has a slight sheen of perfectly tempered chocolate. A hand breaks off one piece — the snap is sharp and clean, the sound of quality chocolate. The camera goes macro on the break — perfect crystalline structure visible in the cross-section. The piece is left resting. The smell is implied by the steam rising very faintly from where it was broken. Deep browns and warm wood tones. One spotlight from above. The sound of the snap is everything.
```

**#7 운동화 광고**
```
A pair of white athletic shoes with a distinctive neon green sole sits on a reflective black surface. The shoes glow slightly against the dark background. Particles of light drift past them — the visual language of speed and performance. The camera circles the shoes at low level, then rises. A close-up on the sole reveals the technical tread pattern in detail. The shoe design catches both the overhead key light and a colored side light. Dynamic. Aspirational. Zero gravity.
```

**#8 고급 위스키**
```
A crystal whiskey glass, half-full of amber whiskey with two clear ice spheres. The glass sits on a dark oak bar, a single spot of warm light illuminating it from above, creating a perfect amber reflection on the wood surface. The ice spheres are slowly melting, releasing the whiskey's aroma in micro-wisps of steam. The camera is at the level of the glass surface, looking across. A man's hand enters the frame and picks up the glass — we see only the cuff of a dress shirt, the cufflink. He raises it. The amber light through the glass is extraordinary.
```

**#9 스마트워치**
```
A smartwatch on a white surface, the screen activating to show a health dashboard — heart rate, steps, sleep data appearing in clean animation. The camera transitions to show the watch from the side — ultra-thin profile, premium aluminum case. Then on a wrist — a quick activity sequence, tap navigation, the face changing to show different modes. Everything fast, clean, capable. Crisp white studio light, minimal shadows. Technology as beautiful object.
```

**#10 천연 샴푸**
```
A bottle of natural shampoo, green and clear packaging, placed in a setting of fresh botanical ingredients — herbs, flowers, citrus slices. Water droplets on the bottle catch the light. The cap opens and a stream of the shampoo, rich and slow-moving, is poured onto a wooden spoon — its color and texture are beautiful. Fresh water is sprinkled on the botanicals, creating sparkle. Morning light, clean whites and fresh greens. Natural. Pure. Real.
```

**#11 명품 가방**
```
A structured leather handbag in deep burgundy, sitting on a white marble pedestal in what feels like a high-end boutique. The grain of the leather is extraordinary in macro — the quality evident in every pore. The hardware: gold clasps, the double-stitched handles, the small brand embossed on the leather in gold. The camera slowly circles, then pushes in to macro details: the stitching, the lining visible through the slightly open clasp. Soft diffused light. Classical music from far away.
```

**#12 비타민 드링크**
```
A sleek bottle of green wellness drink stands in a field of fresh ingredients — spirulina-colored powder, fresh ginger, lime, mint. The bottle is opened and poured into a glass — the liquid is a vivid, slightly effervescent green. Ice cubes are dropped in, creating a satisfying pop-fizz. Morning sunlight through the glass creates patterns on the white surface below. A hand picks it up. Audio: the opening of the bottle, the pour, the ice, and the sip. Energy and health in visual form.
```

**#13 고급 호텔 타월**
```
The morning sun enters a luxury hotel bathroom through gauze curtains, falling on a perfectly folded stack of white Turkish cotton towels. The camera moves slowly across the stack, macro-detailing the dense weave and the slight sheen of perfectly laundered cotton. One towel is shaken out in slow motion — it unfurls in perfect white, catching the light. It lands across the edge of the marble bathtub. The tub fills slowly in the background. Luxury, white, simple, perfect.
```

**#14 전기자전거**
```
A premium e-bike on a forest trail at golden hour — the bike gleams in the warm light, the electric blue accents on the frame lighting up. From above: the bike is tiny on the trail, the forest enormous around it. The motor activates — a subtle electric whine — and the bike begins moving. From the front: the headlight illuminates the trail as it picks up speed, weaving slightly with the terrain, the motor's quiet power doing the work. Free. Clean. Future.
```

**#15 예술적인 맥주**
```
A craft beer being poured into a tall glass — the pour angle is perfect, head building slowly to a cream-colored one-finger foam. The bubbles rise through the amber liquid in columns. The glass is backlit, making the beer glow. Macro on the foam: thousands of tiny bubbles, each reflecting a tiny world. The glass is set on a weathered oak bar. A hand wraps around it. The condensation begins forming immediately. Audio: the pour, the carbonation, the satisfaction of the glass on the bar.
```

**#16 여성 향수 (플로랄)**
```
A delicate perfume bottle — pale pink glass, round and feminine in shape — stands surrounded by freshly cut roses and white peony petals on a vanity table in golden afternoon light. The glass catches the light, scattering it into soft rainbow patterns on the table. A drop of perfume is applied to a wrist — the liquid is pale pink-gold. The camera rises to the window: outside, a garden. The sound: nothing except the faintest hint of something that might be music or might be summer air.
```

**#17 프리미엄 헤드폰**
```
Studio-quality over-ear headphones in matte black with subtle gold accents, floating against a deep dark background. They rotate slowly to show the earcup cushion's quality — plush, deep, perfectly formed. The headband's adjustment mechanism is shown in detail — premium metal, precision-engineered. They are placed on a head (from behind, we see only the back of the head) — the fit is immediate, perfect. The person is still. They close their eyes. The music starts. We feel it.
```

**#18 스마트 홈 기기**
```
A modern living room at dusk. A voice activates a small cylindrical smart device on the coffee table — its ring glows blue to confirm. The lights in the room respond, warming to a perfect evening setting. The blinds close automatically. Music begins. The temperature display changes. All around the room, devices come to life or adjust — a demonstration of a connected home, seamlessly, quietly, perfectly. The person in the chair exhales and relaxes.
```

**#19 수제 가죽 지갑**
```
A slim handmade leather wallet being crafted — stitching needle going through pre-punched holes in vegetable-tanned leather, the thread pulled taut, the stitch perfectly even. Close up: the leather's texture, the thread's tension, the craftsperson's hands moving with absolute confidence. The finished wallet placed on the work table: perfect, minimal, made to last decades. Warm workshop light, the smell of leather implied, the sound of thread through leather.
```

**#20 미래형 스피커**
```
A cylindrical premium speaker on a minimalist shelf — white, seamless, with a barely visible mesh surface. It powers on silently, a very faint pulse of light traveling upward through the body. Music begins — and we see the speaker's response: the entire body vibrates subtly with bass frequencies, visible only in the lightest reflection changes. The sound fills the room — we see it in the way the potted plant's leaves stir slightly, in the single curtain that moves, in the warmth that seems to fill the space. Sound made visible.
```
