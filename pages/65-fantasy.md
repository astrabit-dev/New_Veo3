# 55. 판타지 단편 영화 템플릿

## 목표

판타지 장면은 VEO 3의 능력이 가장 빛나는 영역 중 하나입니다. 마법 효과, 드래곤, 고대 건축물, 서사적인 조명 — 이 모든 것을 단 하나의 프롬프트로 실현할 수 있습니다.

이 템플릿이 효과적인 이유:

- **영화적 스케일**: 수천만 원짜리 CGI 효과를 단 몇 크레딧으로 구현
- **포트폴리오 가치**: 고품질 판타지 클립은 클라이언트 유치에 효과적
- **유튜브 친화적**: 긴 영상의 오프닝이나 하이라이트 클립으로 활용
- **확장 가능**: 연속 클립으로 단편 영화 제작 가능

### 공식

```
[캐릭터 묘사] + [마법/판타지 요소] + [서사적 조명] + [웅장한 배경] + [카메라 움직임] + [음악적 분위기]
```

## 완성 예시 1: 마법사 결투 장면

### 시나리오
두 강력한 마법사가 고대 성탑 꼭대기에서 최후의 결투를 벌이는 장면입니다.

### 전체 프롬프트

```
Two powerful wizards face each other on top of an ancient crumbling stone tower at the edge of a cliff, at dusk. The first wizard — an old man with a long white beard, white and gold robes, staff crackling with golden lightning — stands on the left. The second — a younger woman with silver hair, black and purple robes, her hands wreathed in swirling dark energy — faces him from the right. Lightning bolts of opposite colors — gold and violet — arc between them, meeting in a spectacular explosion of energy at the center. The stone floor cracks and shatters beneath their feet. Storm clouds swirl dramatically overhead, colored purple and orange by the setting sun. The tower begins to crumble at the edges. Camera orbits slowly around the two figures, revealing the vast landscape of mountains and valleys far below. Epic orchestral score swells, emphasizing the final moments of the duel. Cinematic grade, HDR, photorealistic magic effects, cinematic scope ratio.
```

## 완성 예시 2: 드래곤 조우 장면

### 시나리오
홀로 산 정상에 선 기사가 거대한 드래곤과 처음으로 눈을 마주치는 장면입니다.

### 전체 프롬프트

```
A lone knight in full gleaming silver armor stands on a rocky mountain peak above the clouds, back to the camera, looking out at the horizon at sunrise. Suddenly, from the golden clouds below, an enormous dragon rises — its scales are deep crimson and black, each the size of a shield, its wingspan shadowing the entire mountainside. The dragon hovers in the air before the knight, at eye level, close enough that its breath — wisps of smoke and embers — drifts toward the knight's visor. The dragon's eyes are intelligent, ancient, glowing amber. For a long moment, neither moves. The knight slowly raises one gauntleted hand — not in threat, but in greeting. The dragon tilts its massive head slightly, studying the figure. Golden sunrise light bathes the entire scene from behind the dragon, creating a divine halo effect. The wind from the dragon's wings moves the knight's cape dramatically. Sweeping cinematic drone shot slowly pulling back to reveal the full scale of the mountain and dragon. Epic orchestral score, no dialogue, pure visual storytelling.
```

## 완성 예시 3: 고대 예언 장면

### 시나리오
선택된 자가 고대 신전에서 예언의 빛을 받는 순간, 그의 운명이 밝혀지는 장면입니다.

### 전체 프롬프트

```
Inside a massive ancient temple chamber, a young warrior kneels at the center of a glowing circular rune carved into the floor. The chamber is enormous — towering stone columns disappear into darkness above. Suddenly, a beam of pure white light descends from a single circular opening in the ceiling, directly onto the kneeling figure. As the light touches him, the runes on the floor begin to glow, spreading outward in rings of golden energy. Ancient carvings on the walls light up in sequence, depicting a story — wars, a dragon, a sacrifice, a rebirth. The warrior raises his head, eyes wide, filled with reflected golden light. Tears stream down his face not from sadness but from overwhelming revelation. Other monks and scholars around the edges of the chamber step back in awe. The camera slowly pushes in from a wide establishing shot to a tight close-up of the warrior's illuminated face. Orchestral choir swells. Dusty particles float in the beam of light. The entire scene feels like destiny being fulfilled. Epic, emotional, cinematic.
```

## 판타지 영상 품질 올리는 키워드

- `cinematic scope ratio` — 영화관 비율(2.39:1)
- `HDR lighting` — 높은 다이나믹 레인지
- `photorealistic magic effects` — 현실적인 마법 효과
- `epic orchestral score` — 웅장한 오케스트라 음악
- `God rays` — 신성한 빛줄기 효과
- `practical and digital effects` — 실사+디지털 합성 느낌
- `IMAX quality` — IMAX급 해상도와 스케일
