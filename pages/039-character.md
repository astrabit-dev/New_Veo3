# 요소 1: 캐릭터 (Character)

> **누가 등장하는가 — 영상의 첫 번째 질문**

---

## 캐릭터 섹션이란

8요소 공식의 첫 번째 요소인 캐릭터 섹션은 영상의 주인공을 묘사합니다. 인물, 동물, 사물, 상상의 존재 모두 캐릭터가 될 수 있습니다.

좋은 캐릭터 묘사는 AI가 구체적이고 일관된 캐릭터를 생성하는 데 도움을 줍니다.

---

## 캐릭터 묘사의 6가지 요소

### 1. 기본 정보
```
나이 + 성별 + 인종/민족 (선택사항)
예: "A 35-year-old Korean woman"
    "An elderly Japanese man"
    "A teenage boy"
```

### 2. 신체적 특징
```
체형 + 키 + 피부색 + 머리카락 (색상, 길이, 스타일) + 눈
예: "tall and slender, with olive skin, short-cropped silver hair,
    and sharp grey eyes"
```

### 3. 복장
```
상의 + 하의 + 신발 + 액세서리 + 특징적인 아이템
예: "wearing a worn leather jacket over a white shirt,
    dark jeans, boots, with a silver pocket watch chain"
```

### 4. 얼굴 특징
```
두드러지는 특징 (수염, 흉터, 안경, 주근깨 등)
표정의 기본 상태
예: "with a short neatly trimmed beard, small round glasses,
    a thoughtful expression"
```

### 5. 자세와 몸짓
```
서 있는 방식, 걷는 방식, 손 위치
예: "standing with crossed arms and slightly tilted head,
    exuding quiet confidence"
```

### 6. 문화적/사회적 맥락
```
직업, 신분, 역할을 암시하는 요소
예: "battle-worn samurai", "corporate executive", "street artist"
```

## 캐릭터 유형별 예시 10개

### 1. 탐정 (Detective)
```
A sharp-featured woman in her late 30s, shoulder-length auburn hair,
wearing a charcoal trench coat over a navy blouse,
small silver detective's badge pinned to the lapel,
perceptive green eyes scanning the scene,
professional yet world-weary demeanor
```

### 2. 판타지 전사 (Fantasy Warrior)
```
A massive orc warrior, 7 feet tall, dark green skin,
battle-scarred face with a broken tusk,
wearing heavy black iron armor etched with tribal symbols,
holding a war axe twice his size,
battle-ready and fierce expression
```

### 3. 현대 사무직 (Office Worker)
```
A 42-year-old man in a slightly rumpled grey suit,
loosened tie, reading glasses pushed up on his forehead,
brown hair with hints of grey at the temples,
tired but determined expression, briefcase in hand
```

### 4. 어린이 (Child)
```
A 7-year-old girl with wild curly red hair and freckles,
wearing yellow rain boots and a blue raincoat,
holding a small yellow umbrella,
wide curious brown eyes, gap-toothed smile
```

### 5. 빅풋 (Bigfoot)
```
Bigfoot, approximately 8 feet tall, covered in dark brown fur,
holding a smartphone, wearing a tiny camera hat,
surprisingly expressive eyes visible in the fur,
lumbering through snowy forest
```

### 6. 로봇 (Robot)
```
A sleek humanoid robot with chrome and titanium body,
glowing blue optical sensors,
one arm raised in a friendly wave gesture,
subtle LED patterns running along the torso
```

### 7. 노인 어부 (Elderly Fisherman)
```
A weathered 75-year-old fisherman, deep sun-tanned skin,
crow's feet around kind grey eyes,
wearing a yellow rubber slicker and a battered captain's hat,
pipe in the corner of his mouth, calloused hands
```

### 8. 신비로운 마녀 (Mysterious Witch)
```
A pale woman of indeterminate age with waist-length black hair
streaked with silver, wearing layered dark robes with golden embroidery,
half-smiling with amber cat-like eyes,
a raven perched on her shoulder
```

### 9. 우주 비행사 (Astronaut)
```
An astronaut in a white space suit with the visor raised,
revealing a determined 30-year-old face,
small flags on the shoulder, mission patch on the chest,
floating in zero gravity, looking both awed and focused
```

### 10. 스트리트 댄서 (Street Dancer)
```
An 18-year-old breakdancer mid-spin,
wearing a bright orange hoodie and baggy pants,
sneakers with LED soles glowing,
expression of pure joy and concentration
```

## 비인간 캐릭터 묘사 팁

동물이나 상상의 존재를 묘사할 때는 감정과 표현을 의인화해서 넣으면 더 매력적인 영상이 됩니다.

**예시:**
```
A majestic silver wolf sits on a snow-covered hill,
its golden eyes filled with ancient wisdom,
head raised slightly as if listening to distant sounds,
frost breath visible in cold air
```

## 캐릭터 묘사를 위한 빠른 참고 키워드

**체형:** slender, athletic, stocky, petite, towering, round
**머리카락 색:** jet black, auburn, platinum blonde, salt-and-pepper, fiery red
**머리카락 스타일:** close-cropped, shoulder-length, waist-long, braided, messy bun
**눈 색상:** ice blue, amber, emerald green, dark brown, mismatched
**피부:** pale, olive, sun-tanned, dark bronze, fair
**표정:** stern, gentle, playful, melancholic, determined, exhausted

풍부한 캐릭터 묘사가 영상의 감정적 연결을 만드는 시작점입니다.
