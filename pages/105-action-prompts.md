# 87. 액션·어드벤처 프롬프트 20선

## 액션 프롬프트 작성 원칙
- 움직임을 구체적으로 묘사 (속도, 방향, 물리적 효과)
- 환경이 액션을 방해/지원하는 방식 묘사
- 클라이맥스 순간을 명확히 지정
- 카메라가 액션을 따라가는 방식 지정

---

**#1 사막 추격전**
```
A lone rider on a black horse gallops at full speed across a vast sun-baked desert, kicking up massive dust clouds behind. Three masked pursuers on lighter horses gain ground from the right. The rider banks sharply left, cutting across a dried riverbed. The lead pursuer draws alongside, their horses running neck-and-neck. Dramatic low-angle tracking shot, golden desert light, thundering hooves on the audio track, dust filling the frame.
```

**#2 빌딩 사이 파쿠르**
```
A young woman in athletic gear runs at full speed across a rooftop in a neon-lit city at night, reaches the edge and launches into a flying leap across a wide gap to the next building. She lands in a forward roll and immediately transitions into a sprint. The camera follows her from behind in a low tracking shot, then cuts to a wide aerial shot showing the vast cityscape below. Motion blur on her clothing, city lights reflecting on wet rooftops.
```

**#3 수중 전투**
```
Two divers in black wetsuits engage in a close-quarters struggle twenty meters below the ocean surface. Blue filtered light from above illuminates the fight in shifting patterns. One diver disarms the other of a diving knife, sends it spiraling away into the deep. Air bubbles stream upward from their breathing apparatus. Slow-motion, underwater acoustics, coral reef visible in the soft-focus background.
```

**#4 열차 지붕 액션**
```
A man in a long coat fights his way across the roof of a speeding train cutting through mountain scenery at dusk. Wind tears at his coat. He dodges a blow from his opponent and grabs a signal pole for balance. The mountains blur past at speed behind them. Camera orbits them from a helicopter angle, revealing the scale of the mountain pass and the speed of the train below.
```

**#5 우주 전투 조종석**
```
POV from inside a fighter spacecraft cockpit during an intense dogfight in the asteroid belt. The pilot's gloved hands work the controls rapidly. Through the HUD display, three enemy ships bank in formation around a massive asteroid. A laser bolt grazes the right wing — the cockpit rocks violently. The pilot pulls into a steep inverted dive behind a floating rock formation. Stars spin in the canopy.
```

**#6 고성 침입**
```
A thief in all-black tactical gear rappels silently down the stone wall of a medieval castle at midnight. The rope barely makes a sound against the ancient rock. She pauses on a window ledge, checks the courtyard below — two guards walking patrol. She times their movement, then swings through the window. Inside: treasure room, moonlight on gold. She smiles and gets to work.
```

**#7 바다 위 격투**
```
Two fighters exchange blows on the deck of a ship in the middle of a violent storm. Rain pours down, waves crash over the railing, the deck tilts dramatically. They slide and grab rigging to maintain balance mid-fight. Lightning illuminates the fight in strobing white light. The ocean stretches in all directions — nowhere to run. Handheld camera, water everywhere, epic storm audio.
```

**#8 정글 덫 탈출**
```
An explorer in worn khakis races through dense jungle as an ancient stone mechanism activates behind her — a rolling stone sphere as tall as she is, crashing through the undergrowth. She ducks under a vine, leaps a gorge, barely stays ahead. The jungle floor ahead gives way to a ledge overlooking a waterfall and valley far below. She skids to a halt at the edge. The stone sphere thunders up behind her.
```

**#9 얼음 동굴 전투**
```
A warrior in Viking-era armor battles a giant ice troll deep inside a glacial cave. The troll's fists slam the ice floor, sending shockwaves of frost outward. The warrior rolls aside and drives a spear into the creature's shoulder. Blue-white bioluminescent crystals in the cave walls light the scene. Their breath steams. The cave begins to crack around them from the force of the fight.
```

**#10 드래곤 기마 전투**
```
A rider in full plate armor astride a golden dragon swoops down from the clouds toward a battlefield below. The dragon breathes fire in a sweeping arc — the enemy cavalry breaks and scatters. The rider draws a longsword, the dragon banks hard right, brings them alongside a fortress wall. Epic wide angle reveals the full scale of the battle below. Orchestral war theme, wind rushing past.
```

**#11 다리 위 마지막 대결**
```
Two swordsmen meet at the center of an ancient stone bridge over a misty gorge at dawn. They circle each other slowly, studying. One lunges — the fight erupts in a flurry of steel on steel. They fight back and forth across the narrow bridge, trading ground. A sword is knocked away and falls into the gorge below. The disarmed fighter catches a dagger from his belt. The duel continues.
```

**#12 번개 속 비행**
```
A winged figure in dark armor flies through the heart of a massive electrical storm. Lightning strikes all around — some close enough to feel the heat. The figure weaves between the bolts, riding the air currents. One wing catches a glancing strike — sparks fly but the flight continues. Below, a city of lights in the rain. Above, more clouds and more lightning. Dramatic, elemental, unstoppable.
```

**#13 모터사이클 고속 추격**
```
A motorcycle weaves at extreme speed through a mountain switchback road in a heavy rainstorm. Headlights pierce the rain. A black SUV pursues closely, nudging the motorcycle's rear wheel. The rider cuts inside a hairpin turn that the SUV cannot match, gaining distance. But the road ahead narrows as it approaches a bridge under construction. Dramatic night rain, tire screech on wet asphalt.
```

**#14 고대 신전 탈출**
```
An archaeologist sprints through a crumbling temple as the ceiling begins to collapse. Stone blocks the size of cars fall around him. He slides under a falling column, vaults over a collapsing floor section. Torch light illuminates the path forward. Behind him, the passage fills with falling stone. Ahead — a crack of daylight from the temple entrance. He pushes his legs harder.
```

**#15 마지막 보스 결투**
```
The hero — battered, armor cracked, one arm hanging — faces the dark sorcerer in the center of a circular chamber. The sorcerer floats six feet off the ground, energy crackling around him. The hero raises their sword with their remaining good arm. The sorcerer unleashes a devastating energy bolt — the hero deflects it with the sword, the energy arcs back. The chamber shakes. Everything is on this moment.
```

**#16 잠수함 심해 추격**
```
Two submarines — one sleek and black, one military grey — maneuver through the crushing dark of the deep ocean. The black sub rolls to avoid a torpedo — it clips the hull and veers away. The crew visible through portholes, moving fast. Another torpedo launches. This one tracks perfectly. The black sub dives deeper, into the trench, where no light reaches. The torpedo follows. Into the dark.
```

**#17 눈보라 속 탐험**
```
A mountaineer in heavy cold-weather gear pushes against a screaming blizzard near the summit of an impossibly high peak. Every step is a battle against the wind. She reaches a handhold on the final rock face — her ice axe bites in. The summit flag is ten meters above, barely visible in the white. Her oxygen mask fogs with each breath. She reaches for the next hold.
```

**#18 도시 옥상 저격**
```
A sniper prone on a rain-slicked rooftop high above the city, eye to scope, waiting. The crosshairs settle on a target three blocks away. Rain drops run down the rifle barrel. The spotter beside her counts down quietly. The wind changes — she adjusts. One breath in. Half out. Hold. The target pauses in a doorway, illuminated briefly by the lobby light behind them.
```

**#19 용암 도주**
```
A geologist runs from a collapsing volcanic mountain as lava flows down the path behind her. The ground is hot — her boots smoke with each step. She leaps across a crack in the earth, lava visible far below. A helicopter circles overhead — the crew drops a rope ladder. She grabs it with both hands as the lava reaches the spot where she stood a second before. The helicopter banks away.
```

**#20 마지막 전차 돌격**
```
A single World War II era tank rolls forward alone across a devastated European battlefield at dusk. Smoke from burning farmhouses fills the horizon. The tank's gun tracks left and fires — a distant explosion. The tank commander stands in the turret hatch, surveying through binoculars. Ahead, the bridge to the city. Behind, the rest of the unit still miles back. The tank rolls on alone into the smoke.
```
