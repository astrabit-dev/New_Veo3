# 58. 공포·스릴러 장면 템플릿

## 목표

공포 영상은 VEO 3에서 만들 수 있는 가장 긴장감 있는 콘텐츠 유형 중 하나입니다. 올바른 조명, 카메라 각도, 소리 조합으로 등골이 서늘한 공포 장면을 만들 수 있습니다.

이 템플릿의 활용 사례:

- 공포 유튜브 채널 클립
- 할로윈 특집 콘텐츠
- 단편 공포 영화 장면
- 스릴러 영상의 예고편

### 공식

```
[어두운 조명 + 그림자 활용] + [더치앵글 또는 로우앵글] + [으스스한 소리/침묵] + [느리고 의도적인 움직임] + [위협 요소의 암시]
```

### 공포의 4원칙

| 원칙 | 설명 | 프롬프트 적용 |
|------|------|--------------|
| 무엇을 보여주지 않느냐 | 위협을 직접 보여주지 않음 | "something moves in the shadows" |
| 소리가 화면보다 앞선다 | 소리로 먼저 공포 조성 | "a sound like breathing echoes from somewhere" |
| 천천히 | 빠른 장면보다 천천히가 더 무섭다 | "camera slowly creeps forward" |
| 공간의 위협 | 공간 자체가 위협적으로 느껴지도록 | "every doorway is an unknown" |

## 완성 예시 1: 빈 집 탐색 장면

### 시나리오
밤중에 오래된 빈 집에 혼자 들어간 인물이 손전등 하나만으로 집 안을 탐색하는 장면입니다.

### 전체 프롬프트

```
A young woman in her 20s explores an abandoned house at 2am, armed only with a small flashlight that creates a narrow cone of white light in the total darkness. She moves slowly through a hallway, the beam of light sweeping across peeling wallpaper with faded floral patterns, a broken mirror on the wall that reflects her light back distorted, and a staircase at the end of the hall. Her breathing is controlled but audible. The camera follows close behind her at shoulder height, slightly unsteady. As she reaches the foot of the stairs, she stops. The flashlight beam catches something at the top of the stairs — a rocking chair, slowly rocking on its own. No wind. No explanation. She stands very still. The rocking chair slows to a stop. Silence. The only sounds are her soft breathing and the distant settling creaks of the old house. She takes one step up the stairs. Then, from the room to her left — a room she already passed — comes the sound of something heavy dragging across the floor, slowly. She freezes. Camera holds on a tight close-up of her face: eyes wide, jaw clenched, deciding. Dutch angle. Deep shadows on all sides. The light flickers once.
```

## 완성 예시 2: 거울 속 존재 장면

### 시나리오
거울 앞에서 일상적인 행동을 하던 인물이 자신의 반영이 따라 하지 않는다는 것을 깨닫는 장면입니다.

### 전체 프롬프트

```
A man stands at a bathroom sink at night, washing his hands, the only light a single bulb above the mirror that creates harsh shadows under his eyes and chin. He dries his hands, looks up at his reflection. Normal. He turns to leave. His reflection does not turn. It remains facing forward, watching him. He stops. Turns back slowly. His reflection is completely still, staring at him from the mirror with the same face but completely different eyes — calm, unblinking, faintly smiling where he is not. He raises his right hand slowly. His reflection raises its left — mirroring correctly. He exhales. Then he lowers his hand. His reflection keeps its hand raised. The camera is positioned beside the mirror at a low angle, capturing both the man and his reflection at the same time, making it unclear which one the viewer is watching. The bathroom is narrow and claustrophobic. Sound: the drip of the tap, the hum of the light bulb, and then — from inside the mirror — the faint sound of breathing that is not his. He reaches out and touches the mirror surface. It ripples like water. Extreme slow motion on the ripple. Horror. Dread. No music.
```

## 완성 예시 3: 밀림 추격 장면

### 시나리오
밤의 울창한 밀림을 전력으로 뛰어가는 인물의 POV 추격 장면입니다. 무엇이 쫓는지 보이지 않습니다.

### 전체 프롬프트

```
Frantic first-person POV footage of someone running at full speed through a dense tropical jungle at night. The person holds a phone for light — the screen barely illuminates a few feet ahead, showing roots, wet leaves, undergrowth flashing past in a blur. The breathing is ragged and desperate, every exhale a half-suppressed scream. Branches whip against the camera. The runner stumbles, falls — camera hits the ground, showing wet mud and leaves from ground level, then scrambles upright. Behind them, through the trees, something moves — fast, large, silent. Not a person. Not an animal. Just a presence in the dark, moving between the trees at impossible speed, always staying just out of the light. The runner does not look back. The sounds: pounding footsteps, the wet slap of leaves, the frantic breathing — and beneath all of it, growing louder, a low vibration in the air that is not quite a sound, felt more than heard. A root catches the runner's foot — they go down hard — camera pointing straight up at the jungle canopy, stars visible through the leaves high above. Everything goes still. The footsteps stop. Silence. Then — directly above the camera — a shadow passes between the stars. Blackout.
```

## 공포 장면 핵심 키워드

**조명:** `single flickering light source`, `deep shadows`, `practical lighting only`, `no ambient light`

**카메라:** `dutch angle`, `low creeping angle`, `tight close-up on eyes`, `camera holds still while subject moves away`

**소리 (매우 중요):**
- `unsettling ambient drone` — 불안감을 주는 저음 드론
- `silence punctuated by single sounds` — 적막 속 하나의 소리
- `distant indistinct whispering` — 먼 곳의 흐릿한 속삭임
- `the sound of something dragging` — 끌리는 소리
- `breathing that is not yours` — 본인의 것이 아닌 호흡 소리

**금지 키워드 (공포를 약하게 만드는 것들):**
- ~~bright lighting~~ — 밝은 조명은 긴장감을 파괴
- ~~fast cuts~~ — 너무 빠른 편집은 공포보다 액션이 됨
- ~~explicit monster reveal~~ — 위협을 보여주는 순간 공포가 반감됨
