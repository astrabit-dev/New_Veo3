# 57. 로맨스·드라마 장면 템플릿

## 목표

감성적인 드라마 장면은 YouTube와 Instagram에서 높은 저장율과 공유율을 기록합니다. VEO 3는 인물의 표정, 감정적인 조명, 부드러운 카메라 움직임을 조합해 실제 영화 같은 감성 드라마 클립을 만들어냅니다.

이 템플릿의 활용 사례:

- 드라마/영화 예고편 스타일 클립
- 음악 영상(MV) 스타일 콘텐츠
- 감성 유튜브 채널 콘텐츠
- 웨딩/기념일 특별 영상

### 공식

```
[황금빛/부드러운 조명] + [클로즈업 감정 포착] + [부드러운 음악 언급] + [감정을 보여주는 구체적 행동] + [환경의 감성적 디테일]
```

---

## 완성 예시 1: 공항 이별 장면

### 시나리오
공항에서 오랜 연인이 헤어지는 장면입니다. 말로 표현하기 어려운 감정을 행동과 표정으로 보여줍니다.

### 전체 프롬프트

```
A couple stands at the departure gate in a busy international airport, surrounded by the noise and movement of travelers but existing in their own quiet world. The man — late 20s, worn leather jacket, backpack — faces the woman — similar age, soft cardigan, eyes glistening. They hold both hands between them, neither wanting to be the first to let go. The woman reaches up with one hand and straightens his jacket collar — a small, intimate gesture that says everything. He catches her hand as she pulls it away and holds it against his chest for a moment. She looks down, then back up, a small sad smile forming. The camera slowly pushes in from a wide shot that shows the busy airport behind them to a close-up that captures just their faces and joined hands. Warm amber lighting from the terminal windows creates a golden glow despite the institutional setting. Soft piano score underneath. Sound design: the distant airport PA system, rolling suitcase wheels on tile, but muffled and distant — the couple's world feels separate. Cinematic drama, no dialogue, pure emotional visual storytelling. Shallow depth of field, warm color grade.
```

## 완성 예시 2: 빗속 재회 장면

### 시나리오
오랫동안 헤어졌던 두 사람이 비가 내리는 거리에서 우연히 재회하는 순간입니다.

### 전체 프롬프트

```
A rain-soaked night on a quiet city street with golden reflections of streetlamps pooling on the wet pavement. A woman in her early 30s with dark hair plastered to her face from the rain stands on the sidewalk, holding a collapsing umbrella that has blown inside out — she didn't notice, too frozen in place. Across the narrow street, a man in a dark coat has just emerged from a doorway and stopped completely still, staring at her. They haven't seen each other in years — this is apparent in the way time seems to stop when their eyes meet. Neither moves for a long, suspended moment. Then, slowly, the woman's expression shifts from shock to something more complex — recognition, sadness, longing, joy all at once. The camera is at street level, shooting between their two figures with the rain falling between them, each in their own pool of streetlight. Raindrops catch the light like diamonds. He takes one step toward her. A melancholic but hopeful string arrangement plays softly. The rain intensifies slightly, as if the weather itself is responding to the moment. Slow motion, emotional, cinematic, golden-teal color grade.
```

## 완성 예시 3: 편지를 읽는 장면

### 시나리오
오래된 집에서 혼자 앉아 옛 연인이 보낸 마지막 편지를 읽는 여성의 장면입니다.

### 전체 프롬프트

```
A woman in her 50s sits alone at a weathered wooden kitchen table in a sunlit, slightly worn farmhouse kitchen. Morning light streams through lace curtains, casting soft patterns on the table. She holds a letter — handwritten on yellowed paper, clearly old — with both hands. Her reading glasses are pushed up on her head, forgotten. As she reads, her expression cycles slowly through emotions: a small smile at first, then something more bittersweet, her eyes becoming glassy. She pauses in her reading, looks out the window at an overgrown garden outside, then back at the letter. Her thumb traces one line of handwriting gently. She folds the letter carefully, as if it might break, and holds it against her heart for a moment, eyes closed. The camera starts outside the kitchen window looking in, then slowly pushes through into a close-up of her face. The sound design is intimate: the quiet creak of the old house, distant birdsong outside, the soft rustle of the paper. A gentle, melancholic piano and strings composition plays almost imperceptibly. The scene is about memory, time, and something that was and is gone. Warm golden morning light, soft focus, deeply emotional.
```

## 드라마 장면 감정 설정표

| 장면 | 핵심 감정 | 조명 | 음악 |
|------|-----------|------|------|
| 이별 | 슬픔 + 사랑 | 따뜻한 황금빛, 차가운 형광빛 대비 | 피아노 단음 |
| 재회 | 놀람 + 그리움 | 빗속 가로등 반사, 황금-청록 | 현악기 멜랑꼴리 |
| 편지 | 추억 + 상실 | 부드러운 아침 자연광 | 피아노+스트링 |
| 고백 | 긴장 + 설렘 | 황금 시간대, 따뜻한 빛 | 기대감 있는 피아노 |
| 용서 | 갈등 + 해소 | 흐린 날 부드러운 빛 | 조용하고 희망적 |

## 감성 드라마 필수 키워드

**조명:** `warm amber light`, `golden hour glow`, `soft window light`, `candle-lit`

**카메라:** `slow push in`, `shallow depth of field`, `rack focus between faces`

**음악:** `soft piano score`, `melancholic strings`, `intimate chamber music`

**색상:** `warm golden color grade`, `golden-teal`, `muted warm tones`, `soft pastel`

**감정 행동:** `traces with fingertip`, `holds against chest`, `looks away then back`, `slight trembling of lips`
